Rev. Eric Armah-Godwin Lead Pastor

Rev. Eric Armah-Godwin is the Lead Pastor of Inter-City Prayer Chain. As an ordained Pastor & Reverend, Rev. Eric has accepted his calling as a revivalist and through this founded: Inter-City Prayer Chain. His hearts desire is to unite the church once again and re-ignite the fire of the Holy Ghost in Christ. He has served in Global Light Revival Church, House of Prayer Ministries, RCCG Plymouth & many others! With a team of partners, Rev Eric is able to carry out Inter-City’s vision.
